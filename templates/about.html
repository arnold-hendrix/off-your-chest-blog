About Me

Oh, so you wanna know.

Hi, my name is Arnold. This website is a result of an assignment I was working on from the 100 Days of Code bootcamp by Angela Yu. Now you may ask, "why were you in a coding bootcamp?" Valid question. Due to an unfortunate series of events, I had to take a semester off, and instead of completing my studies at Seneca College in the winter of 2021, I had to wait until the summer of 2021. So I decided to use that time to work on my programming skills.

I am primarily interested in software development, but I have learned a lot about electronics and hardware programming during my time at Seneca, of which I maintain a keen interest in.

This website used to have random content on the front page, but I have replaced them with articles I have written on Medium. Future articles will be added here as well. Thanks for the visit, and I hope to you see you around.
