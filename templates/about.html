About Me

Oh, so you wanna know.

Hi. My name is Arnold and I am a Computer Engineering student at Seneca College. This website is a result of an assignment I was working on from the 100 Days of Code bootcamp by Angela Yu. Now you may ask, "why were your in a coding bootcamp?" Valid question. Due to an unfortunate series of events, I had to take a semester off, and so instead of completing my studies at Seneca in the winter of 2021, I had to wait until the summer of 2021. So I decided to make use of that time to really work on my programming skills.

I am primarily interested in software development, but I have learnt a lot about electronics and hardware programming during my time at Seneca, which I maintain a keen interest in. For my last semester project, I am working on my very first IoT project which I am very excited about. Details on that and other projects can be found on my Github, which is linked at the bottom of the page.

I hope you enjoy the random content I have posted on the home page. I don't have any specific plans for this website at the moment, but working on it gave me my first major sense of accomplishment as a programmer, and for that reason alone, I will keep coming back to it.
